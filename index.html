About the Event

Qiskit Fall Fest PNC 2025 is a beginner-friendly hybrid event hosted at Prithvi Narayan Campus. From Nov 9–14, participants will learn Qiskit from scratch through two daily sessions:

🏫 Physical Workshops: 10:30 AM – 12:30 PM at Prithvi Narayan Campus 💻 Online Sessions: 7:00 PM – 8:00 PM Nepali Time via Google Meet

The final three days (Nov 15–17) will feature hands-on circuit building and a collaborative hackathon. Additional highlights include guest speakers, project demos, panel discussions, and a closing ceremony on Nov 19. Whether you're new to quantum or ready to build, this event is designed to welcome and empower all learners.

Why Should You Join?

  • 🚀 Learn quantum computing from scratch - no prior experience needed!
  • 🧠 Hands-on workshops and real circuit building using Qiskit
  • 🌐 Hybrid format: join physically or virtually, whichever suits you
  • 🤝 Collaborate with peers and mentors during the hackathon
  • 🎓 Earn a certificate upon full participation
  • 🏆 Compete for prizes and recognition in the final hackathon

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! All sessions are designed for beginners with no prior experience in quantum computing or Qiskit.

Do I need to attend both physical and online sessions?

No, you can choose either format. However, to receive a certificate, you must attend all sessions in your chosen mode—either all physical or all online.

Is attendance required for the hackathon?

Yes. Participation in the hackathon is mandatory for certification and prize eligibility.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Certificates will be awarded to participants who attend all sessions and actively participate in the hackathon.

Where will the online sessions be held?

Online sessions will be hosted via Google Meet. The link will be shared with registered participants.
